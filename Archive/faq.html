Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign up" button in the top right corner of our homepage. You'll need to provide your name, email address, and create a password. Once you verify your email, you can start browsing courses immediately.

Is EduLearn free to use

Creating an account and browsing our course catalog is completely free. Individual courses have varying prices, and we also offer subscription plans that provide access to multiple courses at a discounted rate. Some introductory courses are available for free.

What equipment do I need for online courses?

For most online courses, you'll need a computer or tablet with internet access, a web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge), and speakers or headphones. Some specialized courses may require additional software or materials, which will be listed in the course description

Courses and Enrollment

How do I enroll in a course?

Once you've created an account, browse our course catalog and click on any course that interests you. On the course page, click the "Enroll Now" button. You'll be prompted to complete payment (if applicable), and then you'll have immediate access to the course materials.

Can I preview a course before enrolling?

Yes! Most courses offer a free preview that includes the first lesson or a sample video. Look for the "Preview" button on the course page to get a feel for the instructor's teaching style and course content.

How long do I have access to a course?

Once you enroll in a course, you have a lifetime access to the course materials. You can learn at your own pace and revisit the content whenever you need a refresher. The only exception is subscription-based courses, which are accessiable as long as your subscription is active.

What is your refund policy

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid courses. If you're not satisfied with a course for any reason, you can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. Simply contact our support team through the contact page.

Do you offer certificates upon completion?

Yes! Most of our courses offer a certificate of completion. To earn your certificate, you must complete all course modules and pass any required assessments. Certificates can be downloaded as PDFs and shared on professional networks like LinkedIn

Technical Support

I'm having trouble accessing my course. What should I do?

First, make sure you're logged into your account. If you're still having issues, try clearing your browsercache and cookies, or try accessing the course from a different browser. If the problem persists, contact our technical support team at support@edulearn.com.

Can I download course videos to watch offline?

Currently, course videos are available for streaming only and cannot be downloaded. However, we're working on a mobile app that will allow offline viewing. Course materials like PDFs and documents can be downloaded for offline reference.

What browsers are supported?

EduLearn works best on the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience and security.

Is there a mobile app?

We're currently developing mobile apps for iOS and Android, which are expected to launch in Spring 2026. In the meantime, our website is mobile-responsive and works well on smartphones and tablets.

Payment and Subscriptions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MaterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. For institutional purchases, we also accept purchase orders and bank transfers.

What is the EduLearn subscription plan?

Our subscription plan gives you unlimited access to over 300 courses for a monthly or annual fee. Monthly subscriptions are $29.99/month, and annual subscriptions are $299.99/year (saving you %17). You can cancel at any time.

Can I share my account with others?

Accounts are for individual use only. Sharing login credentials violates our terms of service. However, we offer family plans and team licenses for organizations. Contact us at info@edulearn.com for more information.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Go to "My Account" > "Subscription" > "Cancel Subscription". You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Learning Experience

How long are the courses?

Course lengths vary depending on the subject matter. Most courses range from 2 to 20 hours of content. Each course page displays the total duration and estimated completion time based on recommended pacing.

Can I learn at my own pace?

Absolutely! Our courses are self-paced, meaning you can complete them as quickly or slowly as you prefer. There are no deadlines for self-paced courses (through some instructor-led courses may have scheduled sessions).

Are there live classes or is everything pre-recorded?

We offer both options! Most of our courses are pre-recorded for maximum flexibility. However, we also offer live instructor-led courses and workshops. These are clearly marked in the course catalog as "Live" or "Scheduled".

Can I interact with instructors?

Yes! Each course has a discussion forum where you can ask questions and interact with both the instructor and fellow students. Premium courses also include direct messaging with instructors and scheduled Q&A sessions.

How do quizzes and assessments work?

Many courses include quizzes and assessments to test your knowledge. These are automatically graded and you'll receive instant feedback. You can retake quizzes as many times as needed to improve your score.

Instructors and Content

Who creates the courses?

Our courses are created by expert instructors who are professionals and educators in their fields. Each instructor profile includes their credentials, experience, and student ratings. We carefully vet all instructors to ensure high-quality content.

How often is course content updated?

We regularly update course content to ensure it remains current and relevant. Instructors may add new modules, update videos, or refresh materials. You'll be notified when courses you've enrolled in receive significant updates.

Can I suggest a course topic?

We love hearing from our community! You can suggest course topics by emailing suggestions@edulearn.com. We review all suggestions and consider them when planning new course offerings.

Additional Questions

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students with a valid .edu email address receive a 20% discount on all individual courses and subscription plans. Verify your student status during checkout to receive your discount.

Are courses available in languages other than English?

We're expanding our multilingual offerings. Currently, we have courses available in English, Spanish, French, German, and Mandarin. You can filter courses by language in our course catalog.

Can my company purchase courses for employees?

Yes! We offer corporate training programs and team licenses. Contact our business solutions team at business@edulearn.com for custom pricing and enterprise features.

Still Have Questions?

If you didn't find the answer you were looking for, please don't hesitate to reach out. Visit our contact page to get in touch with our support team. We typically respond within 24 hours.
